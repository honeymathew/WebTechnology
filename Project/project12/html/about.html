About Us

At peakyblinders.com we are passionate about watches and this horological passion extends to the corporate culture at our headquarters in Brooklyn, NY. Our team of experts and aficionados live and breathe watches. We are watch collectors too and appreciate the quality of fine timepieces.

This gives us the buying power to offer authentic, brand new watches at the very best prices.
